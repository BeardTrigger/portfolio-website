About me

Designing systems players feel

I'm a Technical Game Designer with a passion for building the invisible scaffolding that makes games come alive — the systems, loops, and mechanics that players interact with thousands of times without ever noticing they're there.

My career spans the full spectrum: from AAA sports titles at EA to indie mobile prototypes, VR experiences, and award-winning interactive cinema. I'm equally comfortable whiteboarding a progression system, implementing it in engine, and writing the documentation that ships it across a 100-person team.

I thrive in cross-disciplinary environments — bridging the gap between design intent and technical reality, and advocating for the player experience at every stage.
